The World Of VR.

VR or what you call it as the the Virtual Reality is a great invention ,probably regarded as one of the best technologies made in the technical Field.

Virtual reality (VR) is basically a computer technology that uses headsets or sometimes its a combination with physical spaces or multi-projected environments to generate realistic images, sounds and other sensations/experience that helps a user to be physically present in an imaginary or virtual environment.

Who invented VR...

Ivan Edward Sutherland is the person who invented the VR.Sutherland was an American Computer Scientist and also known as the 'Pioneer of the Internet'.He is widely regarded as 'The Father of Computer Graphics'.Ivan has made some valuable contributions in the of development of computer graphics and interactive interfaces.

Sutherland's contribution in the field of Computer Science and in the Invention of the Virtual reality technology is quiet notable.

Why is Virtual Reality such a trend in today's world...

As the world is moving forward in the field of Computer Science,Computer Graphics is also taking rapid strides towards improvement day-by-day.And games are the evidence of the improvements that Computer Graphics have made.You can simply check for yourself,compare a game of the 2000's with that of todays.





But the discovery of VR was something out of the box.Till date we could experience high graphics within the screen.But Virtual Reality gives the user's a fabulous experience to be physically present in the graphical or virtual world.

What do you need to Experience Virtual Reality...

To experience Virtual Reality you will need a nice VR Headset, and a smartphone that supports Virtual Reality (VR).

Augmented Reality(AR).

AR is a kind of Virtual Reality(VR) technology that blends what the user sees in their real surroundings with digital content generated by some computer software or program.
One great and popular game based on AR is the Pokemon GO.
