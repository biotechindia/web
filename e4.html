Go cordless with the new Moto e4 Plus

After a successful launch of the Moto c and the Moto c plus, Motorola recently launched the new Moto e4 plus- A successor of the Moto e3 power which was very popular in India launched last year(2016).

The Moto e4 plus is priced at INR 10,000.

So let us check some of the key features of the Moto e4 plus.

Let's check in...

1.RAM

Moto e4 plus has 3GB of RAM.

2.ROM

Moto e4 plus has upto 32GB of ROM.

3.CPU

Moto e4 plus has a Cortex-A53 1.3GHz Quad core CPU.

4.GPU

Moto e4 plus has a Mali-T720 GPU.

5.Processor.

Moto e4 plus runs a Mediatek MT6737 processor.

6.Camera.

-The device has a front camera of 5MP with LED Flash.
-And has a rear camera of 13MP with Autofocus and LED flash alongwith 720p video recording.

7.Battery

Moto e4 plus has a Massive 5000mAh battery.

8.Other features

-Supports fast charging.
-Fingerprint sensor mounted at the front.
-Runs Android 7.1.1 (Nougat) Stock android.

So, Moto e4 plus is a brilliant smartphone ,especially for Stock Android Lovers.
