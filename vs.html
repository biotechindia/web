Moto C+ VS Redmi 4.

Both these smartphones are excellent in terms of performance and features. But Biotech will help you find a better choice amongst the two.

The Moto C+ is available in only one variant however the Redmi 4 is available across Three Variants.So we will compare the variants of the Same price.
