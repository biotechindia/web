India ka Smartphone-Jiophone



India has experienced a revolution in telecommunication in the past 18 months. With the introduction of 4G in India, the telecommunication network in India has made rapid strides towards progress.

In this development of telecommunication in India, one company has played a very crucial role and that is Reliane Jio.

Reliance Jio's eye-catching offers has caught everyone's attention, with free phone calls and data usage at a really affordable cost has really made 4G afforadable to the common masses.

Mukesh Ambani's Jio has provided surprises to the Indian smartphone users. Now Ambani has launced the JioPhone the smart-feature phone for India that will change the face of Indian telecommunication for the world.

Let's check some key features of the Jiophone:



1.RAM The Jiophone has a 512 MB RAM.

2.ROM The Jiophone has 4GB of in-built memory and is Expandable upto 128GB of memory.

3.Processor The Jiophone has a Snapdragon 204 Processor.Note: The Snapdragon 204 is the lowest supported version to support 4G VoLTE.

4.Camera The device has a 2MP Camera.

5.Battery The Jiophone is powered by Li-ion 3.7v 2000 mAh battery.

6.Screen Resolution The screen resolution of the Jiophone is 240 X 320px.



When can you buy the Jiophone?


The Jiophone will go on pre-booking from 24th August and it will be available for free of cost!. However consumers will have to pay INR 1,500 as refundable security charges for over a period of three years. (But the 1,500 rupees will be refunded to you only when you return your Jiophone after 3 years).
