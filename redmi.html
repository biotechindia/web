Xiaomi Redmi 4
A Perfect Smartphone at a Budget price

Xiaomi is a chinese OEM company which has strengthened its grip in the Indian market,in the past 1 or 2 years.

With its speciality to provide brilliant hardware at budget rates to the masses,Xiaomi has become a prefered choice by everyone in India.

Recently the smartphone and technology company launched another smartphone that has caught everyone's attention with some really good specs.And it is,

The Xiaomi Redmi 4

After a Successful launch and a positive response after the launch of Redmi 4A Xiaomi launched the Redmi 4 in May 2017 in India.The model was launched across 3 variants;

  1. 2GB+16GB at Rs.6,999
  2. 3GB+32GB at Rs.8,999
  3. 4GB+64GB at Rs.10,999

Let's take a look at the features:-

1.RAM:

The device is available at RAM's of 2GB,3GB and 4GB.

2.ROM:

The device is again available at ROM's of 16GB,32GB and 64GB.

3.Processor:

All three variants are powered by Qualcomm MSM8940 Snapdragon 435 processor.

4.CPU:

The smartphone has a Octa-core 1.4 GHz Cortex-A53 CPU.

5.GPU:

These phones have Adreno 505 GPU's.

6.CAMERA:

The phone has a front camera of 5MP.
And has a rear camera of 13MP with 1080p HD recording.

7.BATTERY:

All the models have a 4100 mAh Li-Po battery(i.e Non-Removable)

8.Other Specs:

-These devices are expandable upto 128GB of storage.
-Has a Corning Gorilla Glass.
-Has a Fingerprint Sensor.
-Runs MIUI 8 with Android 6.0.1 Marshmallow.
So,these were some of the key features of Xiaomi Redmi 4.
A handy buy for First time buyers.
