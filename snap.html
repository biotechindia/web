Snapdragon Chipsets:
History-
Snapdragon Chipsets are SoC's (i.e System on a chip). Snapdragon processors are manufactured and marketed by Qualcomm.In November 2005 the company announced that it was developing the Scorpion CPU. And in November 2006 the Snapdragon Soc which contained the Scorpion processor. The main idea behind Qualcomm naming these chipsets as 'Snapdragon' is because according to a spokesperson from Qualcomm "Snap and Dragon sounded fast and fierce". Qualcomm is off and running from its success since then.
